##  About

**Capital Guard** is a smart Money Management & Risk Management assistant designed for traders who want to execute trades faster while keeping their risk under control.

Simply enter your **Entry**, **Stop Loss (SL)** and **Take Profit (TP)** prices and Capital Guard will automatically calculate the appropriate **Lot Size** based on your desired **Dollar Risk** or **Percentage Risk**.

It also provides instant order execution, pending order management, daily risk limitation, account statistics and quick trade management.

# User Guide

<div align="center">
<img src="https://github.com/shahabodin121/Capital-Guard-EA/blob/main/pic/ca22.jpg" width="30%">
</div>



## 1️⃣ Instant Orders

Open **Buy** or **Sell** market orders instantly without defining Stop Loss or Take Profit.

While the Buy and Sell buttons remain active, the **Entry**, **SL** and **TP** prices automatically follow the live market price.

---

## 2️⃣ Dollar Risk

Specify your desired risk amount in **Dollar**.

Capital Guard automatically calculates the proper lot size based on:

- Dollar Risk
- Entry Price
- Stop Loss Distance

---

## 3️⃣ Lot Size

Enter your preferred lot size manually.

---

## 4️⃣ Percentage Risk

Specify your desired risk as a percentage of your account balance.

The lot size will be calculated automatically.

---

## 5️⃣ Entry Price

Enter the desired Entry price and press **Enter**.

The assistant automatically determines the order type.

### For Buy

| Entry Position | Order Type |
|---------------|------------|
| Above Market | Buy Stop |
| Below Market | Buy Limit |

### For Sell

| Entry Position | Order Type |
|---------------|------------|
| Above Market | Sell Limit |
| Below Market | Sell Stop |

---

## 6️⃣ Stop Loss (SL)

Enter the Stop Loss price.

- Buy → SL must be below Entry.
- Sell → SL must be above Entry.

---

## 7️⃣ Take Profit (TP)

Enter the Take Profit price.

- Buy → TP must be above Entry.
- Sell → TP must be below Entry.

---

## 8️⃣ Show

Displays Entry, SL and TP levels directly on the chart.

Press again to hide them.

---

## 9️⃣ Trade Direction

Switch between:

- **For Buy**
- **For Sell**

The assistant validates all prices according to the selected direction.

---

## 🔟 Reset

Restore all settings back to default.

---

## 1️⃣1️⃣ Balance

Displays the current account balance.

---

## 1️⃣2️⃣ Collapse Panel

Collapse the lower section of the assistant.

Press again to restore it.

---

## 1️⃣3️⃣ Close All Trades

Close every open position in the trading account.

---

## 1️⃣4️⃣ Close Symbol Trades

Close only the positions of the current chart symbol.

---

## 1️⃣5️⃣ Daily P/L

Displays today's Profit/Loss in **Percentage**.

---

## 1️⃣6️⃣ Monthly P/L

Displays the current month's Profit/Loss in **Dollar**.

---

## 1️⃣7️⃣ Maximum Daily Risk

Set the maximum daily risk limit.

Once the maximum allowed daily risk is reached:

- Further trades are automatically closed.
- **OVER TRADE** warning appears.
- New trades are blocked until the next trading day.

> Open positions are also included in the daily risk calculation.
